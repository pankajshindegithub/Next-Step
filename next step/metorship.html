Mentorship Program

Provide students with guidance from industry
professionals to help them navigate their careers
and personal development.

Mentorship Types

One-on-One Mentorship

Each student is paired with a mentor from the industry, focusing on personalized career goals, challenges, and skill gaps.

Group Mentorship

Group sessions with multiple students led by a mentor. Discussion topics include career paths, handling job stress, workplace expectations, etc.

Alumni Talks

Regular sessions where successful alumni share their career stories, challenges faced, and provide tips for excelling in placements.

Session Structure

Introductory Session:

Each mentor introduces themselves, followed by goal-setting with the mentee(s).

Ongoing Sessions:

Regular one-on-one or group check-ins every few weeks.

Career Mapping:

Mentors help students map out their long-term career goals.

Technology:

Use platforms like Zoom for virtual mentorship, and Slack or Microsoft Teams for ongoing communication.

Duration: 1 hour per session.

Frequency: One-on-one mentorship happens once every 2-3 weeks. Group mentorship or alumni talks can be held monthly.
