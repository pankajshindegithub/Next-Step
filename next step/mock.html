Session Structure

Pre-session Brienfing:

10-minute briefing on the interview style and role

Mock interviews

Conduct a 30-45 minute mock interview based on the company and job role

Feedback Session:

A 10-15 minute review on strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement, along with suggestions for skill development.

Technology:

Use video conferencing tools (Zoom, Google Meet) for virtual mock interviews, along with integrated feedback forms for instant feedback.

Duration: 1 hour per session.

Frequency:

One-on-one mentorship happens once every 2-3 weeks.
